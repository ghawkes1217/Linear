Module: Linear Independence, Bases and Dimension

In the module below, a collection of vectors in $\mathbb{R}^n$ will appear as row vectors in a matrix. The subspace generated by these vectors will appear on the bottom right. Your tasks include identifying:

  • whether or not the collection of vectors is linearly independent,
  • whether or not a given vector is in the span of a collection of vectors,
  • whether or not given vector forms a basis of a fixed vector space,
  • a basis and dimension for a given subspace.
